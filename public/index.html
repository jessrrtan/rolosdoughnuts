About us

The name "Rolo" comes from the nickname given to the people of the capital of Colombia: Bogotá. As a family of Colombian immigrants, we have found that the beauty of America is in its celebration of different cultures. This is why our mission at Rolo’s Doughnuts, as a family owned and operated business, is to bring that spirit of cultural amalgamation in the form of doughnuts! From the French classic crème brûlée and the Filipino favorite ube to American treats like Nutella and s’mores, our filled doughnuts will bring you joy from unique and familiar flavors alike. Combined with our Colombian flair and hospitality, we hope that you are inspired to bring your own uniqueness to the table—and maybe some of our doughnuts too!
